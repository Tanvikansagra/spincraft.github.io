Products & Services Overview

At SpinCraft, we provide comprehensive manufacturing solutions that integrate advanced foundry technologies, precision machining, and engineering expertise. As part of the SpinCraft Group, our operations are supported by specialized divisions that work together to deliver high-performance industrial components. Each company within the group contributes unique technical capabilities, enabling us to manage the complete manufacturing cycle — from casting and machining to engineering development and advanced component production.

This integrated structure allows SpinCraft to deliver end-to-end manufacturing solutions, ensuring superior quality, reliable production, and consistent performance for industries that demand precision and durability. Through collaboration across our group companies, we offer customers a streamlined production ecosystem that combines innovation, efficiency, and engineering excellence.

Firm-wise Products & Services

SpinCraft Precision Pvt. Ltd.

Products: Precision machined automotive components, bearing components, industrial mechanical parts, engine and gearbox components.

Services: CNC Turning & Milling, precision machining, super finishing, custom component manufacturing.

Spin Craft Strategic Systems Pvt Ltd

Products: Grey Cast Iron components, SG Iron castings, engine blocks and housings, industrial casting components.

Services: Green sand casting, casting design optimization, large-scale casting production.

SpinCraft Foundry Pvt. Ltd.

Products: Large ductile iron castings, complex structural components, automotive and heavy machinery castings.

Services: Lost foam casting, near-net shape casting, advanced simulation and design support.

SpinCraft Powertrain Pvt. Ltd.

Products: Powertrain components, engine development solutions, propulsion systems.

Services: Engineering design, engine optimization, prototype development.
