About Spin Craft

Founded in 1968, SpinCraft is a trusted engineering and manufacturing group with over five decades of industrial experience. From its origins in engine manufacturing, SpinCraft has evolved into a multi-company group offering end-to-end design, manufacturing and precision engineering solutions.

Today, SpinCraft operates through four specialized firms, each focused on a critical domain—foundry, precision machining, powertrain systems, and defence-critical manufacturing. Together, we deliver integrated solutions that meet global standards for quality, performance, and durability.
