About SpinCraft

Spin Craft is a globally recognised engineering and manufacturing powerhouse specialising in high-integrity Cast Iron (CI) and Spheroidal Graphite (SG) Iron castings, precision machining, and advanced industrial solutions. We pride ourselves on a vertically integrated approach—spanning the design, development, and manufacturing of engines and powertrains for the off-road, marine, UAV, and agricultural sectors, as well as mission-critical defence components.

Built on a legacy of engineering excellence, Spin Craft converges advanced foundry technology with state-of-the-art machining infrastructure. Our engineering-driven processes ensure that every component meets the highest standards of reliability and performance. As a preferred partner to global OEMs, we remain committed to continuous innovation, technical precision, and the long-term success of our clients.

Our Legacy & Growth

SpinCraft’s journey reflects continuous innovation, engineering advancement, and manufacturing excellence. From a modest machining setup to a modern, technology-driven manufacturing group, we have consistently evolved to meet the growing demands of global industries.

With decades of experience in casting, machining, and engineering development, the organization has expanded its capabilities through infrastructure development, process automation, and advanced manufacturing technologies — building a strong foundation for long-term industrial growth and global competitiveness.

Our Group Strength

Spin Craft operates as an integrated engineering and manufacturing group with specialized divisions working together to deliver end-to-end solutions — from casting and machining to advanced engineering and industrial development.

Our group structure enables seamless coordination across manufacturing stages, ensuring precision, efficiency, and consistent quality across all operations. This integrated capability allows us to handle complex industrial requirements with reliability and technical excellence.
